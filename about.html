Our Goal

Our goal at RESCUED EATS is to create a sustainable food system by reducing food waste and making a difference in local communities. We strongly believe in the importance of saving food and strive to increase awareness around this issue. Through our platform, we bring together those in need, restaurants, and individuals to contribute to this mission.

Food Donation Program

With our specialized platform, restaurants and food establishments have the opportunity to engage with their local community and make a difference. Our unique approach to reducing food waste involves partnering with restaurants to donate excess food to those in need. By joining our food donation program, restaurant owners are able to demonstrate their dedication to this important cause.
